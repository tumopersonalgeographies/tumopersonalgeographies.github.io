Course Summary: During this workshop, students will take a deep-dive into data-visualization and mapmaking. The course operates through a mix of group design critiques, hand-drawn and digitized sketching lessons, and independent coding projects. In the first week, students are pushed to think critically about types of data representation and the subconscious psychology that underlies them. Then in week 2, students will use visualization tools such as Tableau, R, and D3.js to launch their own research project. Taught by Ethan McFarlin
